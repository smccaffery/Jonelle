What is a Deep Clean?

Our Deep Cleaning service is designed for homes that need extra attention. Whether it's a seasonal refresh or your first time hiring a professional, we go beyond the surface to eliminate hidden dust and grime.

The Deep Clean Checklist:

  • Kitchen: Inside microwave, degreasing stovetop, scrubbing backsplash, and exterior cabinet detailing.
  • Bathrooms: Deep scrub of tile grout, calcium removal from faucets, and disinfecting all hard-to-reach corners.
  • Living Areas: Hand-wiping baseboards, dusting light fixtures/ceiling fans, and cleaning window tracks.
  • Floors: Detailed edging with vacuum and professional steam mopping for a sanitized finish.
