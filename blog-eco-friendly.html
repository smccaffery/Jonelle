Why We Love Eco-Friendly Cleaning Products

Published on May 2, 2026 • By Jonelle

Achieving a sparkling clean home doesn't require harsh chemicals that leave behind strong artificial odors. At Simply Essential Cleaning, we love utilizing eco-friendly cleaning methods whenever possible. Here is why.

1. Better Indoor Air Quality

Traditional cleaning products often contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that can linger in the air long after the cleaning is done, triggering allergies and asthma. Green products rely on natural ingredients that keep your air fresh and safe to breathe.

2. Safer for Pets and Kids

Because children and pets spend a lot of time on the floor and frequently touch surfaces (and then their mouths), using non-toxic products gives you peace of mind knowing they aren't being exposed to dangerous residues.
