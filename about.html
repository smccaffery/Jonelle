About Us

Welcome to Simply Essential Cleaning! I am Jonelle, dedicated to bringing a higher standard of cleanliness to the Southern Niagara region.

I understand that life gets busy. Between work, family, and finding time to relax, maintaining a spotless home can easily fall to the bottom of the priority list. That’s where I come in. I founded Simply Essential Cleaning to give my neighbors their time back, providing reliable, professional, and thorough cleaning services tailored to fit your unique needs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local & Trusted: Proudly servicing Port Colborne, Ridgeway, Crystal Beach, Fort Erie, & Chippawa.
  • Attention to Detail: I treat every home as if it were my own, ensuring a spotless finish.
  • Eco-Friendly Options: I love utilizing eco-friendly cleaning methods whenever possible for your family's health.
  • Tailored Services: From standard maintenance to deep scrubs and home organization, I customize my approach to your space.
