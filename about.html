About Us

Welcome to Simply Essential Cleaning! We are Jonelle and Danny, the team dedicated to bringing a higher standard of cleanliness to the Southern Niagara region.

We understand that life gets busy. Between work, family, and finding time to relax, maintaining a spotless home can easily fall to the bottom of the priority list. That’s where we come in. We founded Simply Essential Cleaning to give our neighbors their time back, providing reliable, professional, and thorough cleaning services tailored to fit your unique needs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local & Trusted: Proudly servicing Port Colborne, Ridgeway, Crystal Beach, Fort Erie, & Chippawa.
  • Attention to Detail: We treat every home as if it were our own, ensuring a spotless finish.
  • Eco-Friendly Options: We love utilizing eco-friendly cleaning methods whenever possible for your family's health.
  • Tailored Services: From standard maintenance to deep scrubs and home organization, we customize our approach to your space.
