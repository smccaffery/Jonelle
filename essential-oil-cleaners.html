Essential Oil Homemade Cleaners

At Simply Essential Cleaning, we believe in the power of natural products. For our clients in Port Colborne, Ridgeway, Crystal Beach, and across the Niagara region, using homemade cleaners infused with essential oils is a wonderful way to keep your home sparkling clean while avoiding harsh synthetic chemicals.

Why Use Essential Oils for Cleaning?

  • Natural Antibacterial Properties: Many essential oils, such as tea tree and eucalyptus, naturally fight bacteria and viruses.
  • Aromatherapy Benefits: Essential oils leave your home smelling naturally fresh and can boost your mood or help you relax.
  • Safe for the Family: Natural cleaners are much safer to use around children and pets than conventional chemical cleaners.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Making your own cleaners reduces single-use plastic waste and prevents chemical runoff into water systems.

Our Favorite Homemade Cleaner Recipes

All-Purpose Citrus Spray

Great for countertops, sinks, and everyday spills.

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 15 drops Lemon essential oil
  • 15 drops Sweet Orange essential oil

Instructions: Combine all ingredients in a glass spray bottle. Shake well before each use.

Peppermint Glass & Window Cleaner

For a streak-free shine on mirrors and windows.

  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1.5 cups white vinegar
  • 15 drops Peppermint essential oil

Instructions: Mix in a glass spray bottle. Spray directly on glass and wipe clean with a microfiber cloth.

Tea Tree Heavy-Duty Scrub

Perfect for sinks, tubs, and tough bathroom grime.

  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons liquid castile soap
  • 10 drops Tea Tree essential oil

Instructions: Mix into a paste. Apply to the surface with a sponge, scrub, and rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Important Tips

  • Always use glass containers for your essential oil cleaners, as concentrated oils can degrade plastic over time.
  • Test any new cleaner on a small, inconspicuous area first before applying it broadly.
  • Shake your bottles well before every use to ensure the oils and water are evenly mixed.
