5 Essential Spring Cleaning Tips

Published on March 10, 2026 • By Jonelle

Spring is the perfect time to open the windows, let the fresh air in, and tackle the dirt and dust that has accumulated over the winter. If you're feeling overwhelmed, here are five essential tips to get you started.

1. Work from Top to Bottom

Always start by dusting your ceiling fans, light fixtures, and the tops of your cabinets before wiping down furniture and finally vacuuming or mopping the floors. This ensures you aren't knocking dust onto already-cleaned surfaces.

2. Declutter First

It's much harder to clean around clutter. Take a weekend to sort through your belongings. Donate what you don't need, throw away trash, and put away winter items to make the actual cleaning process much smoother.
