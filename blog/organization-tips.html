How to Keep Your Pantry Organized

Published on February 17, 2026 • By Jonelle

A well-organized pantry doesn't just look great—it saves you time and money. When you can easily see what ingredients you have, you avoid overbuying and make meal prep much simpler.

1. Empty and Wipe Down

Start by taking absolutely everything out of your pantry. Check expiration dates and throw out anything that has gone bad. Once it's empty, use a mild all-purpose cleaner to wipe down the shelves.

2. Invest in Clear Containers

Decanting dry goods like pasta, rice, flour, and cereal into clear, airtight containers keeps them fresh longer and gives your pantry a uniform, satisfying look. Don't forget to add labels!
