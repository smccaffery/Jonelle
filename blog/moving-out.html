The Ultimate Move-Out Cleaning Checklist

Published on February 10, 2026 • By Jonelle

Moving in the Niagara region is stressful enough without having to worry about scrubbing baseboards on your way out the door. Whether you are trying to secure your rental deposit in Port Colborne or preparing a house in Ridgeway for a new buyer, a thorough clean is essential.

1. The Kitchen Deep Scrub

The kitchen requires the most attention. You need to pull out the refrigerator and oven to clean behind them. Thoroughly degrease the stovetop, range hood, and scrub the inside of the oven and microwave.

2. Don't Forget the Details

It's the small things that property managers look for. Wipe down all the baseboards, clean the window tracks, sanitize the light switches, and dust the blinds.
