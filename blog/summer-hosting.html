Preparing Your Home for Summer Hosting

Published on March 24, 2026 • By Jonelle

Summer in the Niagara region is the season of impromptu gatherings, backyard barbecues, and weekend houseguests. Make sure your home is always ready to impress so you can focus on enjoying the sunshine instead of stressing over the mess.

1. Refresh the Guest Spaces

If you're expecting overnight visitors, give the guest bathroom a thorough scrub. Ensure you have fresh towels out, check that toiletries are stocked, and clear some closet or drawer space for their belongings.

2. Focus on High-Traffic Areas

When hosting, people naturally gravitate toward the kitchen and living room. Declutter your countertops to make room for serving trays, and give your floors a good vacuuming and mopping right before guests arrive.
